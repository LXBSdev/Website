Our Mission

We as an organization have a mission. Just as the internet is for everyone, so are our projects. Everyone can learn from our codes. From our HTML sites to our Minecraft plugins. The keyword is open-source. We want that everyone can see what we are doing and recreate it.

Why?

We want to make a point and show that it is possible to run a organization and still share the code and ressouces with your community. Sharing all of this doesn't mean that someone else will just "steal" it from you. There is still a lot of work associated with all of this and not everyone has the time to recreate all of this. Also this way the community can even help us with problems and solve them on a entire different level as they also have access to all the code. They are also able to see how our projects work and this way it is visible what we are doing, unlike other big companies where you have no idea how they work and what they do with your data etc.
