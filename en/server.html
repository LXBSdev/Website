About our Server

Our server is still under construction.
We are working daily on our Minecraft server,
Discord server and our website.
Our strong team is working as hard as possible.
However, we ask for your understanding.
At the moment there are still many things that need work.
There might be some bugs. We ask you not to get upset about problems,
but report them. You can reach us at support@lxbs.de, or on our Discord.
We are also working on a feedback form for the website.
If you want to have fun on our Minecraft server,
feel free to join our Discord Server and just join lxbs.online.

The Future

In the future there will be a wonderful lobby, in version 1.20.
Of course you can join with versions below.
From the lobby you can access our build server and all other games that are planned.
We don't want to reveal too much about this yet.
But we can already say that there will be many classic games as well as some originals.
Some will only be available for selected versions, but the classics will be available for all versions.
