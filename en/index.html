About

LXBS is a organization that hosts a Minecraft Network. Our network can be reached over the IP lxbs.online. We host different Minigames, popular clasics as well as originals.

Our community spot is our Discord Server, here you can chat with others and talk about our Network.

We also have a big open-source base. All our codes are online and publicly visible on our GitHub page. You can also read more about this on our developer page. The community can also help us and suggest features on our Discord or at dev@lxbs.online.
